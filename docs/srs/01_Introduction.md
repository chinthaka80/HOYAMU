# SRS Chapter 1: Introduction & Scope

## 1.1. Technical Introduction
HOYAMU.NET is an enterprise-scale digital platform designed to connect people, businesses, services, products, properties, jobs, and information through a unified ecosystem. 

This document defines the software requirements needed to design, develop, deploy, and maintain the platform.

---

## 1.2. Purpose of the Document
The purpose of this document is to define all functional and technical requirements for HOYAMU.NET. It acts as the single source of truth for:
- **Developers:** Building backend controllers and frontend layouts.
- **UI/UX Designers:** Crafting Figma design assets.
- **QA Engineers:** Designing unit and load test scripts.
- **DevOps Engineers:** Setting up Hetzner Cloud clusters and Docker pipelines.
- **Project Managers & Investors:** Tracking development milestones.

---

## 1.3. Scope of the System
The platform aggregates the following modules under a unified UX:
* **Marketplace:** General classified ads.
* **Business Directory:** Verified Yelp-style listings.
* **Jobs Portal:** Recruiter boards & CV builders.
* **Properties & Vehicles:** Specialized classified portals.
* **Travel & Tourism:** Local hotels & travel guides.
* **Interactive Maps:** Radius search pins.
* **AI Search Engine:** Fuzzy search suggestions.
* **Admin Dashboard:** Moderation and billing configurations.

---

## 1.4. Key Terms & Definitions
- **User:** Any general visitor browsing listings.
- **Merchant / Business:** A registered entity with a business page.
- **Listing:** A published ad (general, job, property, vehicle).
- **Verified Seal:** A validation badge awarded after business document verification.
- **Moderator:** Admin staff reviewing pending listings.

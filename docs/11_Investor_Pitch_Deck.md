## 🛝 Slide 2: The Problem
* **Market Fragmentation:** Users have to hop between 5 different websites to find classifieds, look up vacancies, search for property, discover auto garages, or read local news.
* **Friction & Security:** Existing platforms have outdated UX grids, lack localized district mapping, offer no verified member checks, and are exposed to scam listings.
* **No Unified Search:** No intelligent search helper that understands local conversational queries (e.g., "Prius under 8 million Colombo").

---

## 🛝 Slide 3: The Solution
* **The Super App Approach:** HOYAMU.NET merges Classifieds, Business Directories, Jobs, Properties, and Marketplace into one premium, high-performance web platform.
* **Sanctum Verification:** Mandatory phone and email OTP checks to eliminate fake profiles.
* **HDL UI Guidelines:** Beautiful, responsive client viewports tailored for mobile touch targets.
* **AI Search Gateways:** Natural language fuzzy search translating colloquial queries into database criteria.

---

## 🛝 Slide 4: Market Opportunity
* **Demographics:** Over 22 million citizens in Sri Lanka migrating to digital platforms for jobs, real estate, and commerce.
* **Unserved Ad Spend:** Local businesses seeking targeted directories to post sponsored deals without paying steep marketplace premiums.
* **Ecosystem Gaps:** A premium directory that connects both localized jobs searchers and business directory listings.

---

## 🛝 Slide 5: Product Overview & Mockups
* **Landing Portal:** Interactive hero search dashboards.
* **Search Filters:** Fine-grained pricing, city, category, and verification state filtering criteria.
* **Business Directory Profiles:** Logos, descriptions, timetables, and verified badges.

---

## 🛝 Slide 6: Technology Stack
* **Frontend:** Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS v4, shadcn/ui.
* **Backend:** Laravel 12 (REST API, Sanctum auth key validation).
* **Database:** PostgreSQL with composite indexes, soft deletes, and UUID lookup tables.
* **DevOps:** Docker containers, Nginx reverse proxy, and Cloudflare CDN.

---

## 🛝 Slide 7: Business Model & Monetization
* **Premium Featured Ads:** Enhanced top ranking for classified ads.
* **Subscription Packages:** Basic, Business, and Premium tiers for business directory profile pages.
* **Verification Badges fee:** Single fee for document validation.
* **Job Board Applications:** Featured job vacancy postings.

---

## 🛝 Slide 8: Competitor Analysis
* **Competitor A (Classifieds only):** High traffic but poor UX, no business directory, and no AI features.
* **Competitor B (Directories only):** Static listings, no jobs board, and no classified ads.
* **HOYAMU Advantage:** Complete super platform integration, modern HDL design system, and Sanctum security.

### Chapter 2: Business Overview
HOYAMU.NET is a multi-service portal aggregating Jobs, Property, Vehicles, Business Directory, and Classified Ads. It is built as a unified user experience to minimize user drop-offs and maximize organic SEO traffic capture.

### Chapter 3: The Problem
Digital discovery in Sri Lanka is highly fragmented across separate platforms, leading to poor search experience, lack of verified listings, and a massive digital gap for local SMEs who cannot afford custom web design.

### Chapter 4: Our Solution
A "5-in-1 Super Platform" powered by instant fuzzy search (Meilisearch) and automated AI translations. It connects listings, locations, and supplementary services (like leasing/spare parts for vehicles) on a single results page.

### Chapter 5: Market Opportunity
Over 15.5 Million internet users (72% penetration) and a rapid shift of corporate budgets from print media to digital advertising networks.

# Chapter 6: Market Analysis & Customer Segmentation

## 6.1. TAM, SAM, and SOM Analysis

To understand the scale of our opportunity, we divide our target addressable market (TAM) in Sri Lanka based on total digital ad spend and directory subscriptions:

### 1. Total Addressable Market (TAM)
- **Definition:** The entire digital advertising, recruitment listing, and business promotion market in Sri Lanka.
- **Estimated Size:** **LKR 3,500,000,000 annually**.
- **Scope:** Includes all online ads, social media campaigns, print-to-digital conversions, job placements, and B2B listing software.

### 2. Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
- **Definition:** The digital marketing, merchant directory, real estate marketplace, and job listing advertising spends of local SMEs and corporate firms.
- **Estimated Size:** **LKR 1,200,000,000 annually**.
- **Scope:** Targets local businesses looking for direct customer leads and listing-based conversions.

### 3. Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
- **Definition:** The market share HOYAMU.NET plans to capture within 3 years of launching.
- **Estimated Size:** **LKR 125,000,000 annually** (Approx 10% of SAM).
- **Scope:** Driven by premium business profile subscriptions, B2B recruiter portals, and high-volume featured listing classified ads.

## 6.3. Market Drivers & Trends

1. **Mobile Commerce & Payments:** The massive growth of LankaQR and digital banking apps has prepared Sri Lankan consumers for transacting online.
2. **Shift to Digital Ads:** High newsprint costs have driven local merchants away from printing paper directories and newspapers, shifting listings directly onto search portals.
3. **Intent-rich Local Search Volumes:** High volumes of Sinhala and Tamil search queries on Google show that users are actively searching for localized, geo-specific services.

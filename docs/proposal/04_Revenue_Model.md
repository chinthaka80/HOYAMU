# Chapter 11: Revenue & Monetization Model

HOYAMU.NET scales its business model on a diversified mixed-revenue framework, ensuring financial stability by avoiding over-reliance on a single stream (like AdSense).

---

## 11.1. Core Revenue Streams

```
                       +---------------------------------------+
                       |       HOYAMU.NET REVENUE MATRIX       |
                       +-------------------+-------------------+
                                           |
         +-----------------+---------------+---------------+-----------------+
         |                 |               |               |                 |
         v                 v               v               v                 v
+-----------------+ +------------+ +---------------+ +------------+ +---------------+
| Premium Listings| |Featured Ads| |B2B Directory  | |Recruitment | | Lead Gen /    |
| (Classifieds)   | |(Home page) | |Subscriptions  | |Packages    | | Commissions   |
+-----------------+ +------------+ +---------------+ +------------+ +---------------+
```

### 1. Premium & Bump-Up Listings (Classifieds)
- **Model:** Pay-per-post for high-volume categories (vehicles/properties) or bump-up packages that move general ads back to the top of category pages.
- **Estimated Pricing:** LKR 500 – LKR 1,500 per listing.

### 2. Featured Homepage Advertisements
- **Model:** High-visibility ad placements directly on the main index pages and primary search results.
- **Estimated Pricing:** LKR 2,500 – LKR 10,000 per week.

### 3. Premium Business Directory Memberships
- **Model:** Monthly subscription plans for local merchants. Provides a blue Verified badge, custom SEO dashboard meta-control, review response widgets, and map search priority.
- **Target Price:** **LKR 3,000 monthly**.

### 4. B2B Recruitment Packages
- **Model:** Paid job postings with candidate CV database access. Automated application parsing alerts.
- **Estimated Pricing:** LKR 5,000 per job post / LKR 15,000 quarterly unlimited.

### 5. Lead Generation & Referral Commissions
- **Model:** Users searching for properties or cars are offered optional bank leasing rates and insurance packages. HOYAMU.NET secures a percentage commission on each qualified customer lead forwarded to partner banks and insurers.

## 11.2. Monetization Targets (Year 2 Stable Phase)

To hit the LKR 500,000+ monthly net revenue target, we aim for the following merchant subscription conversions:
* **Directory Subscriptions:** 300 active businesses on LKR 3,000/month plans = **LKR 900,000/month**.
* **Featured Ads:** 50 weekly vehicle/property featured slots at LKR 3,000 = **LKR 600,000/month**.
* **AdSense & Programmatic networks:** Global CPM margins on high traffic volume = **LKR 300,000/month**.
* **Recruitment Packages:** 20 corporate active hiring accounts at LKR 10,000/month = **LKR 200,000/month**.
* **Total Gross Revenue Estimate:** **LKR 2,000,000 monthly**.

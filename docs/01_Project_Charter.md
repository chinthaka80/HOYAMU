# Project Charter

### Project Name
HOYAMU.NET

---

## Tagline
හොයන හැමදේම එකම තැන (Find Everything. One Place.)

---

## Vision
To become Sri Lanka's most trusted digital platform where people can search, discover, connect, and grow.

---

## Mission
To simplify searching, buying, selling, advertising, and discovering information through one modern digital platform.

---

## Goals
- Build Sri Lanka's largest marketplace.
- Create a trusted business directory.
- Develop an AI-powered search platform.
- Help businesses grow digitally.
- Deliver a fast and secure user experience.

---

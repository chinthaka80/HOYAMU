## 🏢 About the Company
HOYAMU.NET is a digital technology startup focused on building Sri Lanka's largest digital super platform. Our mission is to combine fragmented online marketplaces into a single, high-performance ecosystem where users can list products, discover verified local businesses, search jobs, buy/sell properties and vehicles, and read local market news.

---

## 🎯 Vision & Mission
* **Vision:** To become Sri Lanka's most trusted, secure, and widely used digital super platform.
* **Mission:** To connect buyers, sellers, employers, and businesses through advanced web technologies, local-first search filters, and premium security frameworks.

---

## 💻 Tech Stack
* **Client Layer:** Next.js (TypeScript, Tailwind CSS v4, shadcn/ui).
* **API Layer:** Laravel 12 (Sanctum security token auth).
* **Data Layer:** PostgreSQL (UUIDv4 primary keys, composite indexing, soft deletes).
* **DevOps Layer:** Docker, Nginx reverse proxy, and Cloudflare CDN integration.

---

## 📈 Key Differentiators
1. **Unified Super Platform:** Five portals (Classifieds, Directory, Jobs, Properties, Vehicles) in one unified account interface.
2. **Sanctum Verification:** Eradicates spam and scam listings through phone and email OTP checking.
3. **Conversational AI Search:** Semantic parsing translating Sinhala, Tamil, and English queries into database search parameters.

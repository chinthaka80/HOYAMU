## 2. Interaction Specifications
- **Vehicle Comparator:** Users select checkboxes on listing cards to load comparative attributes (spec comparison grid).
- **Auto-Suggestions:** Dropdowns are dynamically updated based on the selected vehicle make.
- **Mobile optimization:** Sidebar collapses into a float filter trigger button.

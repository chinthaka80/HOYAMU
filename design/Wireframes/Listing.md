## 2. Interaction Specifications
- **Show Phone Number Click:** Logs an analytics event and displays the hidden phone number.
- **Apply Bank Lease Click:** Opens a modal containing a form to capture leasing leads.
- **Image Slider Thumbnails Click:** Updates the active preview image.

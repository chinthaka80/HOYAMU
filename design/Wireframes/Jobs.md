## 2. Interaction Specifications
- **Apply Now Click:** Opens a modal containing a form to capture CV uploads.
- **Filter Jobs Submit:** Refreshes job listings list asynchronously.
- **Mobile layout:** Sidebar collapses into a float filter trigger button.

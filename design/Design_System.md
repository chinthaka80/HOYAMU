# HOYAMU Design Language - Overview

## 1. Introduction
The purpose of this design system is to maintain a consistent, premium, and professional user experience across the entire HOYAMU.NET platform. Every page, component, and interaction must follow this design language to prevent layout discrepancies.

---

## 2. Design Philosophy
The HOYAMU Design Language is built on the following aesthetic values:
- **Premium & Elegant:** Visual choices should feel high-end, clean, and modern.
- **Minimalist & Clean:** Prioritize negative space and typography over heavy borders or distracting elements.
- **Fast & Responsive:** Interfaces should feel lightweight, loading and transitioning instantly.
- **Friendly & Usable:** Maintain simple layouts that allow users of all technical levels to navigate with ease.

---

## 3. Brand Personality
- **Trustworthy:** Transparent reviews, verified business checkmarks, and clear interface cues.
- **Simple & Reliable:** Complex actions (like ad posting or CV building) are divided into clean, multi-step wizards.
- **Sri Lankan Identity:** Fully optimized layout supporting Sinhala, Tamil, and English.
- **Innovative:** AI search indicators and smart filters integrations.
